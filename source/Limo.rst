**Limo**
=================

.. toctree::
   :hidden:
   :maxdepth: 1
   :glob:

   Limo/GazeboLimoSim




LIMO is a mobile AgileX robot with innovative multi-modal, compact, and customizable AI modules and open-source ROS packages, which enables education, researchers, enthusiasts to program and develop AI robots easier. In line with strong perception sensors and Nvidia Jetson Nano, making it a better platform to develop more indoor and outdoor applications.
