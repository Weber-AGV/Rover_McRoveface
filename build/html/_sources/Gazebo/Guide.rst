Navigation
==============

This is the list of essential aspects to navigating a Gazebo environment


*Zoom*

You can zoom in and out of your gazebo environment by turning the middle wheal on the mouse. Depending on where the mouse is located, is where it will zoom in and out from

*Change View*

To change the view of gazebo, left click and hold, while dragging the mouse to the new view.
